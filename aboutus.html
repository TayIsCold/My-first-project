Welcome to Shwe Restaurant

There are 4 food shops throughout the Myanmar. Its principal trading brands are Shwe and Lon May. The Group also operates Pub restaurants and a Concessions business which trades principally at Yangon, Mandalay, Bagan, Ngwe Saung and Chang Tha Beaches. Shwe Restaurant is also known as one of the best known ethnic restaurants in Myanmar. The brand has five branches in total, each branch contains its own uniqueness atmosphere, which targets its own customers.

Our Services
